Contact Us

Covenant Pages was created to connect people in Escambia and Santa Rosa to Christian businesses. Our mission is to provide a directory of businesses run by members of faithful Protestant churches. To be listed, a business owner must be a formal member of a church that practices church discipline and that adheres to the Apostles' Creed and Five Solas.
